Kevin

Kevin Systrom (@kevin) is the CEO and co-founder of Instagram, a community of more than 1 billion who capture and share the world's moments on the service. He is responsible for the company's overall vision and stragety as well as day-to-day operations.

Since the beginning, Kevin has focused on simplicity and inspiring creativity through solving problems with thoughtful product design. As a result, Instagram has become the home for visual storytelling for everyone from celebrities, newsrooms, and brands, to teens, musicians and anyone with a creative passion.

Prior to founding Instagram, Kevin was part of the startup Odeo, which later became Twitter, and spent two years ay Google working on products like Gmail and Google Reader. He graduated from Stanford University with a BS in Management Science & Engineering and servea on the boards of Walmart and KCRW.
